Taco Trivia

When did Taco first appear in the United States?

Jeffrey M. Pilcher , taco historian and Professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of Tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magzine)
